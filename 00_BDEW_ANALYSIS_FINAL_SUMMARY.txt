================================================================================
TECHNICAL NOTES
================================================================================

Profile Scaling Methodology:
- Scale Factor = Target Annual Energy / Profile Annual Energy
- Applied uniformly to all 8,760 hourly points
- Preserves load shape, adjusts magnitude
- Example: Node 1 (19,973 kWh) scaled to G4-A (22,000 kWh) = 0.908 factor

Error Calculation:
- Relative Error = |Profile Energy - Target Energy| / Target Energy × 100%
- Measures deviation from ideal match
- Lower is better

Network Switching:
- "0-sw" = with automatic switching
- "1-no_sw" = without switching
- Minimal practical difference for demand-side matching
